# Alyssa's Spanish Rice

* 2T oil
* 2 cloves garlic
* 1C washed rice
* 1.75C vege stock
* 1T tomato paste
* 1t salt
* plenty of pepper

1. Heat oil in large pot
2. Fry garlic and rice until lightly browned
3. Add stock, tomato paste, salt and pepper. Bring to the boil and then simmer for 15-17 minutes until cooked
